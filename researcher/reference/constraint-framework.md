# Constraint Framework

Based on the three growth levers framework. Every business bottleneck maps to one of these.

## The Three Constraints

### 1. Not Enough Customers

The business can't get enough people in the door.

**Common signals:**
- Under 16 Google reviews (below trust threshold)
- No website
- No Google Business Profile or poorly optimized one
- No online booking
- Missed calls with no follow-up
- No Google Ads or SEO effort
- Invisible on search results
- No social media presence at all

**What to sell them:**
- Website build
- Google Business Profile optimization
- SEO and AI search readiness
- Missed-call text-back system
- Online booking setup
- Review generation system
- Google Ads management

---

### 2. Not Enough Revenue Per Customer

The business has customers but isn't making enough money from each one.

**Common signals:**
- Under $60/hr equivalent pricing
- Flat-rate pricing regardless of home size
- No packages (just "cleaning")
- No frequency-based discounts (weekly vs bi-weekly vs monthly priced the same)
- No upsells (deep clean add-ons, fridge, oven, windows)
- Offering too many service types (residential + commercial + carpet + windows)
- No quote form or calculator
- Competing on price instead of value

**What to sell them:**
- Pricing coaching session
- Package design and creation
- Quote calculator / estimator tool
- Offer restructuring (Grand Slam Offer framework)
- Niche-down strategy
- Value-stacking guidance

---

### 3. Losing Clients

The business gets customers but can't keep them.

**Common signals:**
- High review count but recent reviews are declining
- Negative reviews mentioning consistency ("different cleaner every time")
- No visible recurring program
- No client follow-up system
- No automated reminders
- Constant Indeed postings for the same roles (turnover signal)
- No client communication beyond the clean itself
- Low response rate to reviews (don't care or don't notice)

**What to sell them:**
- Client follow-up automation
- Review generation and response system
- Recurring booking system
- Client communication platform
- Hiring and cleaner vetting system
- Quality control and checklist system
- Coaching on client retention

---

## Two Special Cases

### Just Getting Started
- LLC filed within last 12 months
- Under 10 reviews
- Minimal or no online presence
- May not have insurance, background checks, or proper setup yet

**Don't sell them advanced tools.** Give them the must-have checklist and tell them what to build first. They're not ready for AI. They need foundations.

### Ready to Scale
- 4.8+ rating with 100+ reviews
- Packages exist and pricing is solid
- Website works and booking is online
- Clients are recurring
- Strong across all three levers

**The constraint is the owner.** They ARE the system. Everything runs through them. They need AI, automation, and delegation to grow without breaking.

---

## How to Identify THE Constraint

Most businesses have problems in all three areas. Your job is to find the primary bottleneck.

**The test:** "If this business could only fix ONE thing, what would unlock the most growth?"

- If they have no visibility, nothing else matters. You can't retain clients you never got.
- If they have visibility but can't make money, more clients just means more underpaid work.
- If they have clients and revenue but keep losing people, they're on a treadmill.

Fix the bottleneck first. The others improve downstream.
